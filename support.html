Support

To get support, give recommendation or report bugs, please join our Discord server.

Public Support:

If you have general questions or need basic help, you can ask in the #public-support channel.

Tickets (Advanced/Unsolved Issues):

If your issue isn’t resolved in public support or you need personalized assistance, you can open a ticket in the #tickets section. A team member will guide you directly.

Bug Reports:

If you encountered a bug in any of our plugins or modpacks, you can report it in the #bugs channel. Make sure to include logs, screenshots, and steps to reproduce the issue for faster resolutions.

Recommendations:

If you have any recommendations related to our plugins, modpacks, or new project ideas—or if you want to suggest improvements to existing projects—you can share them in #recommendations.
